Why Share A Bite?

We aim to reduce food waste, support local communities, and make it easier for people to help each other. Every request is a story, and every contribution makes a difference.

Where we operate

Share A Bite currently focuses on selected regions in Greece. As the community grows, we plan to reach even more areas.

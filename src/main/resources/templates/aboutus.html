About Us

Share A Bite is a community-driven initiative that connects people who want to give with those who need it most. Our goal is to reduce food waste and support local kitchens by collecting and redistributing uncooked food items. Through the power of collaboration, every small act of sharing becomes a step toward a more caring and sustainable world.
